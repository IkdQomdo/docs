---
title: 'Rent a Server'
---

To make your purchase of a server, choose Buy Server at the Main Menu

<Frame>
	![](/images/server-main.png)
</Frame>

For servers, follow the steps and pick your configuration from a range of available components.

<Frame>
	![](/images/server-confirm.png)
</Frame>

After selecting your preferred configuration, you'll encounter the confirmation dialog.

<Frame>
	![](/images/server-pay.png)
</Frame>

To finalize your order, transfer the service cost to the specified address. Upon payment confirmation, your order will be processed within one hour, you will receive your login details via direct message.


## Demo Server

If you're not looking to rent a Node or Server but want to see how the technology works, you can access our Demo Server in the Main Menu.
